Grow Your Knowledge, Sharpen Your Skills.

Hampton Roads DevFest is a technology conference focused on giving software engineers in the region exposure to new technology innovations and practices.

This fun packed conference will showcases numerous topics around breaking technology to expand your toolbox and offer a great place to network with professionals.

    JavaScript Accessibility - Karl Groves

    In this session, Karl Groves will show - by example - the core requirements for an accessible JavaScript widget. By semi-live coding, in vanilla JS, Karl creates an accessible dialog that manages roles, states, properties, and focus so that it is accessible to all users and, in doing so, help attendees understand the important considerations one must make when creating a universally usable widget.

    Speakers:

    Karl Groves

With Power BI you can create powerful reports and interactive dashboards that allow you to visualize your data and make critical decision in real time. This session will show you how to get started with PowerBI.

    Joel Cochran

FAQ

Here are a few frequently asked questions. If you have a question not covered here, email us at info@hrdevfest.org.

  • Why should I attend this event?

    If you are a software developer (or aspiring one) it is a great way to learn about new technologies and gain new skills. It is also a great way to meet and network with others in the community.

  • How can I register for this event?

    Look at the Register section directly to the right of this FAQ. Enter the type and number of tickets you want. Then click Continue. Easy as pie!

  • I am a student, can I attend?

    Absolutely. Contact us at info@hrdevfest.org with proof of your student status and we will send you a discount code for the tickets.

  • Is there a conference code of conduct?

    Yes. Our code of conduct can be found here.

  • Can I submit a talk?

    Please do! Be sure to submit your talk as soon as possible here. If you require T&E and/or a fee please email us at speakers@hrdevfest.org.
    Normal sessions should be 50 minutes, including questions. Call for speakers closes Oct. 5th.

  • Can I submit lightning/ignite talks?

    Yes -- we will adjust our schedule for these talks. Please be sure to submit your talk here and note on it that it is a lightning/ignite talk.

  • Can I volunteer?

    Absolutely! We need volunteers! Please sign up here and we will contact you about helping with this event.

  • Can I sponsor?

    Sure. A great event needs great sponsors. Email us at sponsors@hrdevfest.org and we can talk about sponsorship options.

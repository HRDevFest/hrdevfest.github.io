Nov. 13th & 14th, 2015

Hampton Roads DevFest is a technology conference focused on giving software engineers in the region exposure to new technology innovations and practices.

DevFest does this through its' hands-on workshops and relevant talks. If you are a software professional and have a talk you believe fits this goal, please respond to our call for speakers.

We plan to have an amazing event for Hampton Roads this year!
